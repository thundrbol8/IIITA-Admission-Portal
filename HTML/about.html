The Campus

The campus plan, based on a bird taking flight symbolises a launchpad for students to shoot for the stars. The beautiful 100 acre campus, situated at Deoghat, Jhalwa, designed meticulously on the Penrose Geometry pattern, is being further topped by fine landscaping.

Academics

IIITA maintaining its legacy in the domain of coding stuff and invention, makes endless efforts in making the students inclined towards the field of oif reasearch with its rigrous academic ciricullum making students arriving ICPC Regionals and finals along with grabbing top-notch offers from global IT leaders like Google, Flipkart, Microsoft to name a few.

Socities

The campus plan, based on a bird taking flight symbolises a launchpad for students to shoot for the stars. The campus being an institute of national importance comply with the rules of and have several technical, cultural societies for students welfare. The student bosy, Gymkhana listens for all the issues and disputes between the student community and the admin panel and hence acts as a bridge.

Residence

The beautiful 100 acre campus, situated at Deoghat, Jhalwa, includes Visitors Guest House, Faculty Guest House, VIP Guest House, 5 Boy's Hostels with capacity of 275 inmates each, 3 Girl's Hostels, 60 Residential Quarters of Type I, II, III & IV, Married Scholar Apartments, Students Activity Centre, Health Centre, Squash Courts, Swimming pool, Tennis Courts, Badminton Courts, Pavilion & Playgrounds.

Fests and Events

Excelling in the field of research, development, the wind of competetive environment blows in the domain of organising several fests. The Effervescence, the annual cultural fest of IITA is organised in Odd Semester every year, whereas the technical and sports fests like Aproksha and Asmita are generally held in Even Semesters. These fests and events provide students a platform to showcase their skills publicaly
